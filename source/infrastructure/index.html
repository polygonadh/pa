Infrastructure

Our plant is strategically located in the industrial hub of Maharashtra which is the most industralized state in India, very near to the sea port.

The plant has the capactiy to manufacture 7200 MT per annum of various grades of adhesives, distributing supplies all over India and exports to middle east contries too.

The company has a well established marketing network and with the help of various channel partners, the product is made available within a short span of time to all our esteemed customers all over India and abroad.

The company's head office is located at Chinchwad, Pune (India) and the marketing office is located at the centre of Pune city (India), thereby ensuring smooth activity of the company.
