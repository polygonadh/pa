Company Profile

LIFE LONG BOND of Quality, Product Innovations, Just-In-Time Service with Competitive Pricing.

From its inception in 2004, Polygon Adhesives and Resins Pvt. Ltd. has achieved significant growth by delivering branded adhesives OMBOND, OMFIX and OMCOL to all its discerning customers. Besides having strong presence in wood-working adhesives, the company is making a distinct mark in Industrial adhesives for Lamination, PSA, Side Pasting, Polysol, Paper Tube / Fiber Drum, etc.

Understanding the need of the time, the company has a well established plant at a strategic location near Pune, Maharashtra, to cater to the customer with just-in-time service and competitive pricing. With the latest equipment, in-process-controls and state-of-the-art R&D lab, the company has won customer satisfaction in a short span of time. The company always belives and works on the principle - "Quality comes in... Quality goes out".

Team Polygon expresses sincere thanks to all our customers, especially to carpenters, architects, end users, dealers and industrial users who have shown faith in our various products and given us enormous strength to stand tall with our vision To become a word class producer of speciality adhesives.
